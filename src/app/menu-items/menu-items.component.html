Bhakri is thick roti made with mota atta. This is very common breakfast in gujarat. There are two to three types of bhakri that you will find people making biscuit bhakri,jeera bhakri and masala bhakri. It can be served with tea, curd or sabji/ sev tamatar ki sabji is best side for bhakri. Bhakri is loaded with ghee or white butter and gives your energy for whole day.
